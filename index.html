How does the integration between ExposureShield and LegacyShield work?

Both services share a unified dashboard and authentication system. Your security data helps inform your legacy planning, and you can manage everything from one account with seamless switching between features.

Are the digital wills legally binding?

Yes, our digital wills are created using legally compliant templates and include guidance for proper witnessing and notarization according to your state's requirements. We work with legal professionals to ensure validity.

Can I upgrade from a single service to the Complete Suite?

Absolutely! You can upgrade at any time from your account dashboard. We'll prorate your current subscription and only charge the difference for the remaining period.
