Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about ExposureShield

How does the integration between security and legacy planning work?

Both services share a unified dashboard and authentication system. Your security data helps inform your legacy planning by identifying which digital assets need protection, and you can manage everything from one account with seamless switching between features.

Are the digital wills legally binding?

Yes, our digital wills are created using legally compliant templates that have been reviewed by estate attorneys. We provide guidance for proper witnessing and notarization according to your state's requirements, and we work with legal professionals to ensure validity.

Can I upgrade from a single service to the Complete Suite?

Absolutely! You can upgrade at any time from your account dashboard. We'll prorate your current subscription and only charge the difference for the remaining period. Your data will be seamlessly merged into the Complete Suite.
