Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our pricing and services.

Is the free security scan really free?

Yes! Our basic security scan is completely free forever. No credit card required, no hidden fees. You'll get a comprehensive report of your digital exposure risk with actionable recommendations.

Can I upgrade from the free plan later?

Absolutely. You can upgrade to the Essential plan at any time through our website. Professional and Enterprise plans will be available soon.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept PayPal for the Essential plan. Professional and Enterprise plans will accept additional payment methods when they launch.

How does the family plan work?

Family plans allow you to add additional members under one account. Each member gets individual protection while you manage everything from a single dashboard. Perfect for families or small teams.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. You can cancel your subscription through PayPal. For Professional and Enterprise plans, cancellation options will be available when they launch.

Do you offer discounts for nonprofits or students?

Yes, we offer special pricing for registered nonprofit organizations, educational institutions, and students. Contact our sales team with documentation for a custom discount.
