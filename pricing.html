Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our pricing and services.

Can I switch plans anytime?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and you'll only pay the prorated difference.

Is there a free trial?

We offer a 14-day free trial on all paid plans. No credit card required to start. Experience full protection before committing.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and for enterprise plans, we also accept bank transfers.

How does the family plan work?

Family plans allow you to add additional members under one account. Each member gets individual protection while you manage everything from a single dashboard.

Can I cancel anytime?

Absolutely. You can cancel your subscription at any time through your account dashboard. No hidden fees or cancellation penalties.

Do you offer discounts for nonprofits?

Yes, we offer special pricing for registered nonprofit organizations and educational institutions. Contact our sales team for more information.
