How does the integration between security and legacy planning work?

Security tools protect your current accounts and exposure risk. Legacy planning tools help you organize future access and instructions. The Complete Suite brings both into one unified workflow.

Are the digital wills legally binding?

The platform provides structured templates and guidance. Legal validity depends on your jurisdiction, witness rules, notarization requirements, and how you complete and store documents.

Can I upgrade from a single service to the Complete Suite?

Yes. You can start with Security Protection or Legacy Planning and later upgrade to the Complete Suite to unlock the full integrated feature set.
