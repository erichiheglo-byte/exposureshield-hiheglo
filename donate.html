Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about donating to ExposureShield.

Why should I donate when you already charge for PDF reports?

PDF reports ($4.99) are for users who need professional documentation. Donations support the free services that 95% of our users rely on. Every free scan costs us money in API fees and server costs. Donations ensure we can continue offering essential security checking to everyone, regardless of their ability to pay.

Are donations tax-deductible?

No. ExposureShield is a for-profit social enterprise, not a registered 501(c)(3) charity. Donations are considered personal gifts and are not tax-deductible. We operate this way to maintain flexibility and avoid restrictions that sometimes come with non-profit status.

Can I get a refund if I change my mind?

Donations are generally non-refundable as they are immediately allocated to our operational costs. However, if there was an error (wrong amount, duplicate charge) or exceptional circumstances, contact us within 7 days at donations@exposureshield.com and we'll review your case.

How often should I donate?

We prefer one-time donations over subscriptions to maintain flexibility for both you and us. However, some supporters choose to donate monthly. There's no obligation – give what you can, when you can. Even a single donation makes a meaningful difference.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit/debit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers through our secure payment processor. All transactions are encrypted and PCI DSS compliant. We never store your full payment details on our servers.

Will my donation information be private?

Yes. We never sell, share, or trade donor information. Your name will only appear in our supporters list if you choose that option. All financial information is handled by our secure payment processors. Review our Privacy Policy for details.

Can I donate anonymously?

Yes. During the donation process, you can select "Donate Anonymously." Your contribution will still be counted in our totals, but your name won't appear anywhere. We'll still send you a receipt email (if provided) for your records.

Do you accept corporate donations or sponsorships?

Yes! We welcome corporate support that aligns with our mission. Corporate donors receive special recognition, impact reports, and can sponsor specific projects. Contact us at partnerships@exposureshield.com to discuss corporate giving opportunities.

Important Legal & Compliance Information

  • Voluntary contributions: Donations are completely optional. All core security services remain free regardless of donation status.
  • Business structure: ExposureShield operates as a for-profit social enterprise (LLC). Donations support our mission but are not charitable deductions.
  • No recurring charges: All donations are one-time unless you explicitly choose a monthly option. We don't auto-renew donations.
  • Privacy protected: Donor information is never sold or shared. View our Privacy Policy for complete details.
  • Secure payments: All transactions are processed through PCI DSS compliant payment processors with bank-level encryption.
  • Immediate receipt: You'll receive a detailed donation receipt via email immediately after payment for your records.
  • Financial transparency: We publish annual reports on how donations are used. Contact us for the latest report.
