Refund Policy

Last Updated: January 2024

1. 30-Day Money-Back Guarantee

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all new subscriptions. If you're not satisfied with our service within the first 30 days of your subscription, you can request a full refund.

2. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund:

  • Email us at: refunds@exposureshield.com
  • Include your account email and reason for refund
  • Refund requests must be made within 30 days of purchase

3. Refund Processing

  • Refunds are processed within 7-10 business days
  • Refunds are issued to the original payment method
  • You will receive email confirmation when refund is processed

4. Non-Refundable Items

  • Renewal subscriptions after 30 days
  • Custom development or consulting services
  • Enterprise plans with custom contracts

5. Subscription Cancellation

You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account dashboard. Cancellation will take effect at the end of your current billing period.
