Common Questions About Donating

Why should I donate when you already charge for PDF reports?

PDF reports ($4.99) are for users who need professional documentation. Donations support the free services that 95% of our users rely on. Every free scan costs us money in API fees and server costs.

Are donations tax-deductible?

No. ExposureShield is a for-profit social enterprise, not a registered charity. Donations are considered personal gifts and are not tax-deductible.
